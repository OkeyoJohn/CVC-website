About Our Church

Discover our story, mission, and the values that guide our community.

Our Story

City Vineyard Church was planted in 2005 by a small group of believers with a vision to create a welcoming community where people could encounter God in a real and transformative way. What started as a home fellowship has grown into a vibrant church family impacting Nairobi and beyond.

Over the years, we've seen God's faithfulness as we've navigated challenges and celebrated milestones. Our journey has been marked by a commitment to biblical teaching, authentic worship, and compassionate service to our community.

Today, we continue to grow as a multi-generational, multicultural community united by our love for Jesus and our desire to see His kingdom come in Kenya.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To love God, love people, and serve our city. We exist to make disciples of Jesus Christ who worship passionately, grow spiritually, and serve sacrificially.

Our Vision

To see Nairobi transformed by the love of Christ through a growing network of healthy, reproducing churches and communities of faith across Kenya.

Our Values

We value authentic worship, biblical teaching, compassionate ministry, authentic community, and cultural relevance in sharing the Gospel.

Our Beliefs

The foundational truths that guide our faith and practice.

What We Believe

We hold to the historic Christian faith as expressed in the Apostles' Creed and Nicene Creed. As part of the Vineyard movement, we emphasize:

  • The centrality of Jesus Christ and His kingdom
  • The presence and power of the Holy Spirit
  • Worship as a lifestyle
  • The ministry of all believers
  • Compassionate ministry
  • Culturally relevant mission

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word and the final authority for faith and practice. We are committed to teaching Scripture in a way that is both faithful to the text and relevant to our context.
