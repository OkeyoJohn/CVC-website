Generosity Changes Everything

At City Vineyard Church, we believe giving is an act of worship and a practical way to participate in God's work. Your generosity enables us to:

Support Local Ministry

Fund our worship services, children's ministry, youth programs, and community outreach initiatives right here in Nairobi.

Support church planting efforts and mission work across Kenya and East Africa, spreading the Gospel to unreached communities.

Serve Our Community

Provide practical help through feeding programs, medical camps, and educational support for those in need.

Giving FAQs

Common questions about giving

Will I get a receipt?

Yes, we provide official receipts for all donations for your records and tax purposes. Receipts are emailed automatically for online giving.

Is online giving secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information.

Can I update my recurring giving?

Yes, you can update, pause, or cancel your recurring giving at any time by contacting our finance office or through your online account.

How is my giving used?

We're committed to financial transparency. Annual reports are available showing how funds are allocated across various ministries and expenses.

Can I give to specific projects?

Yes! You can designate your giving to specific ministries, missions, or building projects. Just indicate your preference when giving.

Need more help?

Our finance team is here to help with any giving-related questions. Contact us at giving@cityvineyard.co.ke or +254 700 123 456.
