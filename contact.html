Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Service Times

We have two Sunday services at 8:00 AM and 10:30 AM. Our Wednesday prayer meeting is at 6:00 PM, and youth service is Fridays at 6:00 PM.

Children's Ministry

Yes! We have age-appropriate programs for children during our 10:30 AM service. Nursery care is available for infants and toddlers.

Parking

We have ample parking available on our campus. Greeters will direct you to available spots when you arrive.

Dress Code

Come as you are! You'll see everything from suits to casual attire. We care more about you than what you wear.

Accessibility

Our facility is fully accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. Please let us know if you need any special accommodations.

Getting Connected

Visit our Welcome Center after service, join a small group, or fill out our connection card to get involved in our community.
