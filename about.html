About Our Church

Discover our story, mission, and the values that guide our community in serving Nairobi and beyond.

Our Story

City Vineyard Church was planted in 2005 by a small group of believers with a vision to create a welcoming community where people could encounter God in a real and transformative way. What started as a home fellowship of 15 people has grown into a vibrant church family of over 800 members, impacting Nairobi and beyond.

Over the years, we've seen God's faithfulness as we've navigated challenges and celebrated milestones. Our journey has been marked by a commitment to biblical teaching, authentic worship, and compassionate service to our community.

From our humble beginnings in a rented community hall to our current campus in the heart of Nairobi, God has consistently provided and guided our steps. Today, we continue to grow as a multi-generational, multicultural community united by our love for Jesus and our desire to see His kingdom come in Kenya.

Our Mission & Vision

The heartbeat of everything we do at City Vineyard Church

Our Mission

To love God, love people, and serve our city. We exist to make disciples of Jesus Christ who worship passionately, grow spiritually, and serve sacrificially in our communities and beyond.

"Go and make disciples of all nations..." - Matthew 28:19

Our Vision

To see Nairobi transformed by the love of Christ through a growing network of healthy, reproducing churches and communities of faith across Kenya and East Africa.

"Your kingdom come, your will be done on earth as it is in heaven." - Matthew 6:10

Our Values

We value authentic worship, biblical teaching, compassionate ministry, authentic community, cultural relevance, and the empowering work of the Holy Spirit in sharing the Gospel.

"They devoted themselves to the apostles' teaching and to fellowship..." - Acts 2:42

What We Believe

The foundational truths that guide our faith and practice

Our Core Beliefs

We hold to the historic Christian faith as expressed in the Apostles' Creed and Nicene Creed. As part of the global Vineyard movement, we emphasize the centrality of Jesus Christ and the present work of the Holy Spirit.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word, fully authoritative for faith and practice, relevant to all people in all cultures.

Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, who died for our sins and rose again, offering eternal life to all who believe.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who empowers us for life and service, and gives spiritual gifts to the church.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, make disciples, and demonstrate God's love to the world.

Our Leadership

Meet the team guiding our church community with wisdom and compassion

Pastor John Mwangi

Senior Pastor

Pastor John has been serving at City Vineyard since 2010 and became Senior Pastor in 2015. He holds a Master of Divinity from Nairobi Evangelical Graduate School of Theology and is passionate about expository preaching and mentoring emerging leaders. He is married to Grace and they have three children.

Pastor Sarah Wanjiku

Associate Pastor

Pastor Sarah joined our team in 2012 and oversees our community outreach, women's ministries, and discipleship programs. She has a background in social work and theology. She has a heart for serving the marginalized and empowering women in leadership. Sarah is married to David and they have two children.

sarah@cityvineyard.co.ke

Youth Pastor

Pastor David leads our dynamic youth ministry and young adults group.
