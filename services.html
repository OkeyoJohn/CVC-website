Our Services

Join us for worship, teaching, and fellowship. We have services and ministries for all ages.

Regular gatherings where we worship, learn, and grow together.

Sunday Worship

Join us every Sunday for uplifting worship and practical biblical teaching that applies to everyday life.

What to Expect

Your first visit to City Vineyard Church

Welcome to Our Family

We know visiting a new church can be intimidating, so we want to make your experience as comfortable as possible.

✓ Friendly Welcome

Our greeting team will welcome you at the door and help you find your way around.

✓ Casual Atmosphere

Come as you are! There's no dress code - you'll see people in everything from suits to jeans.

✓ Engaging Worship

Our worship is contemporary and heartfelt, creating space for you to connect with God.

✓ Practical Teaching

Messages are biblical, relevant, and applicable to everyday life.
